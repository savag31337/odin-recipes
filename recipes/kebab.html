Kebab

Description

To make kebabs, marinate cubed meat (chicken, beef, or lamb) in a mixture of yogurt, spices, garlic, and lemon juice for at least an hour. Thread onto skewers, then grill until cooked through. Serve with flatbreads and fresh vegetables. Enjoy!

ingredients

  • Meat (chicken, beef or lamb)
  • Yogurt (for marinating)
  • Garlic (minced)
  • Lemon Juice (freshly squeezed)
  • Spices (e.g., cumin, paprika, coriander, and black pepper)
  • Salt
  • Olive Oil (for moisture)
  • Vegetables (e.g., bell peppers, onions, and cherry tomatoes for skewering)
  • Skewers (metal or wooden, soaked in water if wooden)

Steps

  1. Marinate Meat: Mix yogurt, minced garlic, lemon juice, spices, salt, and olive oil. Marinate cubed meat for at least 1 hour.
  2. Skewer: Thread the marinated meat and vegetables onto skewers.
  3. Grill: Cook on a preheated grill for 10-15 minutes, turning occasionally, until cooked through.
  4. Serve: Enjoy hot with flatbreads and your favorite sauces!
