Cinnamon bun

Description

To make delicious cinnamon buns, start by combining 2 cups of flour, 1/4 cup of sugar, and 1 packet of yeast in a bowl. Warm 1/2 cup of milk and mix in 1/4 cup of melted butter and 1 egg. Gradually add this mixture to the dry ingredients, kneading until smooth. Allow the dough to rise for about an hour. Once risen, roll it out, sprinkle with a mix of 1 tablespoon of cinnamon and 1/4 cup of brown sugar, then roll it up tightly. Slice into individual buns and place them in a greased pan. ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 packet yeast
  • 1/2 cup milk (warm)
  • 1/4 cup butter (melted)
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar

Steps

  1. Mix flour, sugar, yeast; add milk, butter, egg.
  2. Knead dough; let rise 1 hour.
  3. Roll, sprinkle cinnamon and brown sugar, and roll up.
  4. Cut into buns, place in greased pan.
