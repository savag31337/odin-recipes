Hamburger

Description

To make a delicious hamburger, combine ground beef, salt, and pepper in a bowl. Form into patties and grill or pan-fry for about 4-5 minutes per side until cooked to your liking. Serve on toasted buns with your favorite toppings like lettuce, tomato, cheese, and pickles. Enjoy!

ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Burger Buns (sesame seed or your choice)
  • Cheese (optional, e.g., cheddar, American, Swiss)
  • Lettuce (e.g., iceberg or romaine)
  • Tomato (sliced)
  • Onion (sliced, raw or caramelized)
  • Pickles (sliced)
  • Condiments (e.g., ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise)

Steps

  1. Make Patties: Mix ground beef with salt and pepper, shape into patties, and create an indentation in the center.
  2. Cook Patties: Grill or pan-fry over medium-high heat for 4-5 minutes per side. Add cheese in the last minute if desired.
  3. Toast Buns: Lightly toast the burger buns on the grill or in a toaster.
  4. Assemble: Place the patty on the bottom bun, add toppings (lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles), and spread condiments on the top bun.
  5. Serve: Enjoy your homemade hamburger!
